Italia Immersion: Dive into Italian Life

Over the summer I had the opportunity to travel Italy. During my time there I went to Venice, Florence, Rome, and Sorrento. From those locations, I adventured off to places such as Tuscany, Ravello, Capri, and Tramonti. In each place I learned about different things from Italian history. My favorite places were The Vatican, The Duomo di Milano, and the views from the Amalfi Coast. I would love to take the time to show you, my favorites and teach you about what I learned.
